FraudFinder — Helping Seniors Spot Phishing

Stack (planned): Python, FastAPI, Next.js · ML: explainability, simple risk heuristics

AI-assisted tool that highlights red flags in emails and teaches safe habits with simple, step‑by‑step explanations.

Problem

Email phishing targets seniors with confusing, deceptive patterns. Most tools give a verdict but not a clear, teachable explanation.

Approach

Design a walkthrough that shows where risk signals appear (sender, links, wording), score the message, and provide a short tutorial to prevent future mistakes.

Impact

Clear, teachable moments that increase awareness and reduce risky clicks in pilot tests with family/friends.
